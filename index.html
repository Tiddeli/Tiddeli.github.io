Cosplay? Craft?

Cosplay is in short "Costume play". The phenomenon that is now growing rapidly over the world is challenging peoples creativity and ways to express oneself.

One makes a costume, based on an existing character, and compete, takes photos or just hang around with like-minded people and share laughter and adventures.
