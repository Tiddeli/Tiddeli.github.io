Cosplay? Craft?

Cosplay is in short "Costume play". The phenomenon that is now growing rapidly over the world is challenging peoples creativity and ways to express oneself.

One makes a costume, based on an existing character, and compete, takes photos or just hang around with like-minded people and share laughter and adventures.

Wicked and Divine

This makeup is made after one commented on my instagram picture, asking for suggestions and challanges in cosplay-makeup. Since this series had been something I've been quite intrigued of, i dove right in.

The Anarch

As a Larper you sometimes have to come up with characters that is supposed to look nowhere near you, especially if they are a character you will play in a campaign you are known for playing one specific character for a longer time. This style and concept came up after I needed to think what character to play for a couple of times, while the main character was gone from the floor..
